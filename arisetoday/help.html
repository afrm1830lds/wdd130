Believers of Change:

On this page, we care about you. So, we expect only one thing in return, for you to progress. If you have felt alone and overwhelmed, or maybe find yourself thinking, "I'm not capable of anything anymore", arise today. We are looking for people with any illness to get better and give a first step in the right direction. There are many ways to participate. For example, try to share your story briefly in our external daily blog or maybe, you could pick up others' experiences in our highlights section. There are always meaningful things to catch up with what the site does.
