Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this personal website will be to create a free and secure bridge between people with specific health struggles and part of the help they need to overcome their situation.

Audience

My audience has two groups: People having weight or self-esteem problems and physical and emotional local health professionals. Each group will have a child page of its own, and the home page will contain general information about both groups that could lead users, in general, to think about their daily habits by reflecting on others' highlighted stories. The audience will be people older than 16 and from diverse backgrounds and beliefs.

Home page

Why people could be interested in this website (arise today)?

The Reasons
This website has a clear purpose, connecting people so they can have the chance to be more empathic and helpful to one another. Looking at the big picture, and only with a limited local scope, our initial data suggest that more than 60 % of the people that participated in our first polls have experienced an unusual increase in health problems like depression, anxiety, or eating disorders after the pandemic arrived.

Our proposal
Share knowledge and overcome struggles. This web site role is to allow visitors to connect. There are two main sections divided into child pages. Page one is for people looking for help anonymously or someone trying to share their uplifting stories with others. And the second page is where health professionals could share general knowledge about specific health issues, instructions about public health recourses, and some of their contact information.

What can you expect?
The only thing necessary is to show interest and get involved by following all the protocols, which are very user-friendly. This website articulates, edits, and publishes images, text, and hyperlinks combined through the hard work of its moderators. We receive others' information and convert it into blog posts that get uploaded after the person's approval. Also, by observing that all the established community guidelines are fulfilled and by receiving the person's digitally signed disclaimer before posting.

Help Page


Our Purpose

Believers of Change:

On this page, we care about you. So, we expect only one thing in return, for you to progress. If you have felt alone and overwhelmed, or maybe find yourself thinking, "I'm not capable of anything anymore", arise today. We are looking for people with any illness to get better and give a first step in the right direction. There are many ways to participate. For example, try to share your story briefly in our external daily blog or maybe, you could pick up others' experiences in our highlights section. There are always meaningful things to catch up with what the site does. Use the Volunteers link to learn more about public resources, professional testimonies, or local health professional’s information. If there's something urgent you have to talk to someone, please press the red button at the bottom of this page, follow the instructions, and then check out your email. Any information gathered by this website is confidential and will never be revealed to anyone else. In every case, the person submitting materials has to approve and consent to the information through a disclaimer. This is a support page to connect people with similar health issues with not well-known resources, and even other people willing to help.
